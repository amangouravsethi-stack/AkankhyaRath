06 // Realizations

What I Realized

"Love isn’t just about looking out for someone; it’s also about making them feel looked after. I learned that even if you have good intentions, mistakes still matter."

Listening is just as important as talking, understanding is just as important as being understood, and being patient is just as important as showing love.

I realized that relationships can’t grow on guesses. They need talking, trust, effort, and being steady. Life doesn’t always give us second chances, and I learned that the hard way. Those lessons will stay with me, long after this part of my life is over.
